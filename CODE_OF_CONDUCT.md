## Our standards

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Welcome constructive feedback and suggestions.
- Focus on the project (Responsive Design System) and avoid off-topic or promotional content in issues/PRs.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Spam or unrelated advertising.
- Posting others’ private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Reports can be sent to **help@rskworld.in** or **support@rskworld.in**. We will review and respond as appropriate.
